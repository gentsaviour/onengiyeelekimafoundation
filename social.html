OUR SOCIAL ENGAGEMENT PROCESS

01
Event Planning

We begin by identifying key needs in the community and brainstorming impactful event ideas that bring people together.

02
Volunteer Coordination

We engage passionate volunteers, assign roles, and ensure everyone is well-informed and excited for the event.

03
Promotion & Outreach

We promote events on social media, local networks, and partner platforms to maximize community attendance and impact.

04
Impact & Celebration

After each event, we gather feedback, measure our impact, and celebrate the connections and progress made.
