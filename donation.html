Give — Underprivileged Nigerians Need You More Than Ever

Give to the needy today. The COVID-19 pandemic has hit us harder than ever, and low-income families suffer the most. Families struggling to feed cannot provide essential services to their children. Your gift today can help change lives and communities.

Without the generous donations we've received since December 2020 from individuals and corporate donors, our ongoing “Back to School” campaign wouldn’t be possible. These funds have supported over 732 unfortunate children with school supplies, provided scholarships to orphaned girls in secondary school, and allowed us to construct toilet blocks to combat open defecation.

On January 11th, 2022, we began a free nursery and primary school with the aim of offering free education to orphans and less-privileged pupils.

We encourage you to continue supporting our initiatives so we can reach more rural communities. No amount is too small to help.

Helpers Social Development Foundation is a non-profit organization focused on defeating poverty in African countries through education, health, and economic empowerment. Education is a crucial investment in human capital and essential to achieving the SDG 4 goals. Unfortunately, poverty continues to deny access to proper education—especially for women and girls.

Cryptocurrency donations offer a modern, borderless way to support African education and infrastructure. 100% of your crypto donations go directly to our charitable projects.

Thanks to our crypto donors, we launched a free nursery and primary school in Oji-River, providing supplies and scholarships to over 1,508 underprivileged children. We've also built toilet blocks and hosted health outreaches.

Helpers Social Foundation is among the top charities accepting Bitcoin donations. Your support keeps our mission alive.
