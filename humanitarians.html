Humanitarian Impact That Matters

At Onengiye Elekima Foundation, we are committed to transforming lives through acts of compassion, relief efforts, and inclusive development. Together, we uplift communities and build hope for the future.

Why We Serve

Humanitarian work is at the heart of our foundation. From emergency aid and food support to education sponsorship and medical outreach, we believe that every person deserves dignity, care, and opportunity — especially in times of crisis.

We focus on the most vulnerable — children, women, and displaced families. We provide clean water, school support, menstrual hygiene kits, and livelihood training to improve long-term wellbeing.

OUR FOCUS AREAS

Emergency Relief

We respond to disasters, flood victims, and displaced people with emergency food, clothing, and shelter.

Education & Scholarships

We fund school fees, distribute learning materials, and support girls returning to school.

Community Health

Through outreach programs, we offer medical checkups, menstrual hygiene education, and vaccinations.

Every Act of Kindness Counts

Whether you donate, volunteer, or advocate for our work, you're directly supporting a child, a mother, or an entire village. Together, we build resilience and restore dignity. Let's make compassion a way of life.
