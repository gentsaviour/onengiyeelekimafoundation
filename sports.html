EMPOWERING YOUTHS THROUGH SPORTS

Fueling hope, unity, and strength through grassroots football competitions and youth empowerment programs across Nigeria.

About Our Sports Initiatives

Since 2016, the Onengiye Elekima Foundation has used sports to unite communities, empower underprivileged youth, and promote peace across Nigeria. Our flagship initiative, the Unity Cup, gives boys and girls equal opportunity to showcase their talent, grow their potential, and build lifelong values like teamwork and discipline.

Why Sports Matter

  • Builds character and leadership in youth
  • Promotes social inclusion and gender equality
  • Reduces youth restiveness and crime
  • Encourages education through extracurricular activities
  • Unites diverse communities through healthy competition
